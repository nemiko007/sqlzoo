# コンピュータ専攻 vs 芸術専攻の回答者数

**テーブル構造:** `nss(ukprn, institution, subject, level, question, response, sample, score, A_STRONGLY_DISAGREE, A_DISAGREE, A_NEUTRAL, A_AGREE, A_STRONGLY_AGREE)`

質問22に対して'(8) Computer Science'と'(H) Creative Arts and Design'それぞれの回答者合計を表示
```sql
SELECT subject, SUM(response) FROM nss 
WHERE question='Q22' 
  AND subject IN ('(8) Computer Science', '(H) Creative Arts and Design') 
GROUP BY subject
```
